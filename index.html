Adobe Abodes consists of structures made from Adobe, a brick-like material commonly used in desert regions due to its practicality and efficiency. The Adobe houses and buildings are painted white, reflecting the natural color of adobe in real life. It shares a lot of similarities with Pueblo as well.

MEGA City was a Named Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that was added in Chapter 4: Season 2 to the island Asteria. MEGA City was a vast, modern metropolis that resembled a futuristic depiction of Tokyo. Technologically advanced cars and motorcycles could have been found throughout its streets, while buildings across the city were encircled by Grind Rails.

The Doggpound was a Named Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that was added in Chapter 2 Remix on the island Apollo. It is a remixed version of The Agency in the ownership of Snoop Dogg.

Imagine landing at the familiar, imposing structure of The Agency, but this time, it's been completely Snoop-ified! The sleek, somewhat sterile aesthetic of the original was replaced with a much more vibrant and laid-back vibe. You'd likely find graffiti art with a distinct West Coast hip-hop influence adorning the walls, maybe even some lowrider-inspired decorations scattered around. The atmosphere would have been less about clandestine operations and more about a chill hangout spot, albeit one still heavily guarded by Snoop Dogg's crew. You might have even heard some iconic Snoop Dogg tracks playing on in-game radios as you explored the location.
